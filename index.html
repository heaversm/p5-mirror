Distance of reflected object between two parallel plane mirrors

An object placed between two parallel plane mirrors will create infinite reflections, with each mirror acting as a point of reflection for its opposite reflected object.

The horizontal distance an object is from the mirror is equal to the distance between the mirror and its reflection.
